150. Power of Comptroller and Auditor-General to give direction as to accounts Form of accounts of the Union and of the States.—

Effective 1/4/1977

The accounts of the Union and of the States shall be kept in such form as the Comptroller and Auditor-General of India may, with the approval of the President President may, after consultation with the Comptroller and Auditor-General of India, prescribe.
