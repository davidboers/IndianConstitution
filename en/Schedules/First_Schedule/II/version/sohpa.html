II. The Union Territories

As per the State of Himachal Pradesh Act, 1970

Effective 25/1/1971
Name Extent
1. Delhi The territory which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution was comprised in the Chief Commissioner's Province of Delhi.
2. Himachal Pradesh The territories which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution were being administered as if they were Chief Commissioner's Provinces under the names of Himachal Pradesh and Bilaspur and the territories specified in sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966.
3. 2. Manipur The territory which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution was being administered as if it were a Chief Commissioner's Province under the name of Manipur.
4. 3. Tripura The territory which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution was being administered as if it were a Chief Commissioner's Province under the name of Tripura but excluding the territories referred to in Part IV of the First Schedule to the Constitution (Ninth Amendment) Act, 1960.
5. 4. The Andaman and Nicobar Islands The territory which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution was comprised in the Chief Commissioner's Province of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
6. 5. The Laccadive, Minicoy and Amindivi Islands The territory specified in section 6 of the States Reorganisation Act, 1956.
7. 6. Dadra and Nagar Haveli The territory which immediately before the eleventh day of August, 1961 was comprised in Free Dadra and Nagar Haveli.
8. 7. Goa, Daman and Diu The territories which immediately before the twentieth day of December, 1961 were comprised in Goa, Daman and Diu.
9. 8. Pondicherry The territories which immediately before the sixteenth day of August, 1962, where comprised in the French Establishments in India known as Pondicherry, Karikal, Mahe and Yanam.
10. 9. Chandigarh The territories specified in section 4 of the Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966.
