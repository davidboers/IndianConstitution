13. Estimated receipts and expenditure pertaining to autonomous districts to be shown separately in the annual financial statement.—

Effective 26/1/1950

The estimated receipts and expenditure pertaining to an autonomous district which are to be credited to, or is to be made from, the Consolidated Fund of the State of Assam shall be first placed before the District Council for discussion and then after such discussion be shown separately in the annual financial statement of the State to be laid before the Legislature of the State under article 202.
