Influence from other constitutions:

  • United States Constitution of 1789, Art. I, Sec. 3, Cl. 1: Upper house representing the states; elected by legislatures (before 17th Amendment).
  • South Africa Act, 1909: Sec. 25 and Sec. 26 (Nomination of members of the Council of States by the president, election of members by state legislatures) and Sec. 134 (use of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote).
  • Constitution of Ireland of 1937: Art. 18.3 (Nomination of members of the Council of States by the president) and Art. 18.5 (use of proportional representation by means of the single transferable vote).
