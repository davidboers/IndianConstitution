V

Effective 26/1/1950

Form of oath of office for a Minister for a State:—

"I, A.B., do swear in the name of God solemnly affirm that I will bear true faith and allegiance to the Constitution of India as by law established, that I will faithfully and conscientiously discharge my duties as a Minister for the State of..........and that I will do right to all manner of people in accordance with the Constitution and the law without fear or favour, affection or ill-will."
