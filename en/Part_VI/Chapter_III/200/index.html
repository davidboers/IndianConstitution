Influence from other constitutions:

  • United Kingdom: Assent by head of state.
  • United States Constitution of 1789, Art. II, Sec. 7, Cl. 2: Recommendations by the president.
  • British North America Act (now Canada Act), 1867, Sec. 55 and Sec. 90: Reservation of bills for the consideration of the president.
  • Commonwealth of Australia Act, Sec. 58: Recommendations by the president; requirement for assent after bill passed a second time.
