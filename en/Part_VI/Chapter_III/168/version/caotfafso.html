168. Constitution of Legislatures in States.—

As per the Constitution (Amendment of the First and Fourth Schedules) Order, 1950

Effective 26/1/1950; Order effective as of 25/1/1950.

(1) For every State there shall be a Legislature which shall consist of the Governor, and

(a) in the States of Bihar, Bombay, Madras, Punjab, the United Provinces Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal, two Houses;

(b) in other States, one House.

(2) Where there are two Houses of the Legislature of a State, one shall be known as the Legislative Council and the other as the Legislative Assembly, and where there is only one House, it shall be known as the Legislative Assembly.
