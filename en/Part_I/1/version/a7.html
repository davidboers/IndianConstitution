1. Name and territory of the Union.—

Effective 1/11/1956

(1) India, that is Bharat, shall be a Union of States.

(2) The States and the territories thereof shall be the States and their territories specified in Parts A, B and C of the First Schedule as specified in the First Schedule.

(3) The territory of India shall comprise—

(a) the territories of the States;

(b) the territories Union Territories specified in Part D of the First Schedule; and

(c) such other territories as may be acquired.
