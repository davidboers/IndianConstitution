Territories of States

The territory of the State of Assam shall comprise the territories which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution were comprised in the Province of Assam, the Khasi States and the Assam Tribal Areas but shall not include the territories specified in the Schedule to the Assam (Alteration of Boundaries) Act, 1951.

The territory of each of the other States in this Part shall comprise the territories which immediately before the commencement of this Constitution were comprised in the corresponding Province and the territories which, by virtue of an order made under section 200A of the Government of India Act, 1935, were immediately before such commencement being administered as if they formed part of that Province.
